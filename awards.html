Awards

Art Without Walls

A contest organized by Lambda Theta Alpha, contestants submitted their artwork telling their immigration story. Miguel Urrego participated in 2019, and won seccond place.

CMS Art Contest

A contest organized for students K-12 in Mecklenburg county, Miguel Urrego participated in the High School category, and won first place.

Participation

VozEs Magazine

Miguel Urrego was featured in the 2020 edition of VozEs Magazine, a magazine that showcases the work of Latinx artists in the Charlotte area.

VozEs Art Gallery

A gallery organized for Latino Artists by VozEs. Miguel Urrego had his artwork showcased in this gallery for 2 months.
