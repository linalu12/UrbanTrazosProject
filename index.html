Artist Statement


I have always loved to draw ever since I was a kid. It is my way of expressing myself. I enjoy exploring my creativity everyday, and learning new ways to showcase my feelings on paper or canvas.


Through this website, I hope I will be able to share my art with others. If I can inspire at least one person through my art, I will have achieved my mission.
